I wanted to direct you to my short movie online that combats Islamophobia by highlighting the media's role in (mis)representation: Semiotics of Islam: A Primer For Kuffar.

Last week it was featured on Huffington Post as part of "Muslims in America."

You can also check out past coverage of the film on India.Com, Apogee Journal andon Asia Pacific Forum on WBAI.

The film premiered at the Queens World Film Festival in March.
It screened at the Iowa City Documentary Film Festival and won an honorable mention.
Next weekend, it will screen at the Muslim Writers Collective in New York City.

I wanted to know if chapati mystery would be interested in featuring it. Both the actor, Adeeba Rana and I are available for interviews if that helps!

Please feel free to contact me at any time!
