A sweet old lady in the UK did a search on google: "pakistani man marrying british woman what are his right of remaining in uk". My blog was # 3 - first two being a canadian royal site and wikipedia entry on Pakistan. She emailed me asking for help navigating British Immigration channels.
So, I did some googling of my own and found an answer and emailed it to her.
I was gonna make a smartyass comment about google failing her...but it didn't. Google karmically gave her exactly the right link.
Isn't love grand?
