Happy Mother's Day to my dearest Amma who waited and waited, at the doorstep, for me to come home every evening from college.

And also, forbade me from learning to swim in case I drowned. And furthermore, made me drink karayla ka juice until my innards turned green. You are the coolest Amma and I am a terrible son but I love you more than anything else.
